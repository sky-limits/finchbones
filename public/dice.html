Dice Commissions

I can create custom dice, but I also sell pre-made sets. You can see the pre-made dice below, as examples and for ordering purposes. To order, please visit here: Google Form.
If there are any things I cannot do that you ask to be done, I will let you know!

Dice are 100% handmade from resin, and cured in a pressure pot. This process takes 24 hours, and can sometimes go wrong! In the event of imperfect dice, I will remake your order for free. You can request to have the defective dice as well, otherwise I will sell them as a pre-made set.

Dice are shipped out with USPS on Tuesdays and Thursdays.

All dice remain UNINKED until the customer specifies the number color they want; otherwise they can choose to paint it themselves.
