Chapter I: Colter

It's cold. Colder than anything we've ever known, and too many of our friends have died or are dying in front of us. We are powerless to stop it. We only know what Dutch, Hosea, somehow our superiors, tell us to do. A first little twinge of panic - discomfort? Approaches when Dutch refuses to answer about the activity on the boat in Blackwater.

The man of the house, the only one he can depend on.
