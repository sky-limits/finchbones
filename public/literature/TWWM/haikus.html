[twwm] 5A - Haikus

Menri

Cinnamon, and light.

Where is your home, little hare?

What have you lost here?

Polaris

Orchestral grief, loss-

The moon bathes you in her glow,

You forget the hurt.

Shaka

Called to the sea late,

An open horror of night

Letting yourself go.

Koto

Doggedly hold on,

Not letting go of the sled.

You can never leave.

Illuse

Your canvas lies, dark,

Your daughter is lost as well.

Will the dunes welcome?

Pooki

Little raccoon king,

Lording over your garden.

The flowers bow down.
