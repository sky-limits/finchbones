[TWWM] Senseless Illusions

Bundled

Fog gathering in
Bundling senses within
Hiding it from me.

Veins
The rolling in of the clouds called me
It hummed and danced around me
And I knew deep down I was gone.
But the swirling, towering, twirling,
Traipsing, trailing mist hung heavy
In my heart -
It filled my veins -
My mind was painted with the simple,
Shifting colors of the fog, and
I was back.

Asking
Shining, piercing, cyan neon
Bright bright light filled my body.
There was nothing left in me
But
A gentle, soft, invisible,
Almost soundless little whisper.
"Why?"
A screech filled my ears, I threw my arms up-
I attempted to hold my breath in, not let
The sound hold me captive.

Sound
Cold ice filled my lungs,
Surrounded by darkness as I reawoke
And the eyes closing in on me
Echoed,
Left behind,
The eyes in my mind. That
Howling
Whipping
Screaming
Sound that had reverberated into my soul.

??.
A black shape filled my vision as I grew
Coherent?
Mindful?
Scared.
The blood boiling sound came into my world again
Deep breath, I told myself.
There were no words.
It was
Static?
White noise?
Feral.
I had been changed by this shape,
My original form lost behind in,
In swirling threads and wisps of whispers.
Form?
Body?
Gone.

Breath
This was it,
The last step,
The strangest one.
Breathe in, breathe out.
I was watched,
Held pinned under,
Water filling lungs.
Breathe in, breathe out.
The other - esk,
Stood over me,
Eyes shredding soul.
Breathe in, breathe out.
I suffocated there,
My last night,
Heart stopped completely.
Breathe out.
